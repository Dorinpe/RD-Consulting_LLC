Our Mission

Our mission is to power global food security by providing essential refrigeration solutions for food processing, storage, and distribution. We exist to make sure food stays safe and accessible — by engineering refrigeration systems that support every step from harvest to table. Core mission: Build refrigeration technology that keeps food safe from harvest to table—so no one goes hungry because food couldn’t be properly stored or processed.

Positive Social & Environmental Impact

Social Impact: Advancing Food Security

By enabling safe processing, storage, and distribution, our systems help reduce massive post-harvest food losses — supporting global efforts to feed the world and align with UN Sustainable Development Goals like Zero Hunger.

Environmental Impact: Cutting Waste & Emissions

Food loss contributes 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions. Our efficient refrigeration minimizes spoilage, reduces energy use, and supports low-GWP refrigerants — conserving resources and limiting deforestation.
